Types of Dresses

A-Line Dress

Recognized by its A-shaped silhouette, the A-line dress fits snugly at the shoulders and flares out towards the hem. It’s a versatile dress that can be worn for casual outings, work, or semi-formal events

Off the Shoulder

This dress style leaves the shoulders bare and may include a sleeve or ruffle on the bicep. It’s perfect for a date night or a summer party where you want to showcase your shoulders and arms

Shift Dress

With its simple, boxy shape, the shift dress hangs straight down from the shoulders and is usually short and sleeveless. It’s ideal for those who prefer a minimalist look and can be dressed up or down for various occasions, from casual meet-ups to more formal gatherings
